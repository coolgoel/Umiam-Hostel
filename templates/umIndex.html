About Us

Inaugurated by our former Director Sir, Prof. Gautam Biswas, Umiam Hostel was the 8th Hostel in IITG. The origin of name came from the river Umiam originating from Meghalaya. The composition of the Hostel residents is also very rich, including students of Bachelor's, Master's and Doctorate degree. So, in every way this Hostel represents the soul of IIT G
